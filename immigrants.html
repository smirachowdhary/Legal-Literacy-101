Immigrants’ Rights Toolkit

Part 1: If You Are Stopped

  • You have the right to remain silent.
  • You do not have to answer questions about immigration status.
  • You can ask to speak to a lawyer.

Part 2: At Work or School

  • Workers must be paid regardless of immigration status.
  • Schools cannot deny education based on status.
  • You are not required to disclose status.

Part 3: Documents & Emergency Planning

  • Carry only required documents.
  • Keep copies in a safe location.
  • Have emergency contacts prepared.
