About

Legal Literacy 101 exists to make the law clear, accessible, and usable.

Our mission

We provide plain-language, multilingual legal information so immigrants, workers, and students understand their rights and feel empowered to advocate for themselves.

What we believe

Access to legal knowledge should not depend on income, language, or background. Everyone deserves to understand the rules that affect their lives.
